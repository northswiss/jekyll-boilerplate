How does TravelPerk make money?

When you book your travel using TravelPerk, we earn an affiliate commission and are paid a small percentage of the cost of travel from the airline or hotel meaning there is no additional cost or fees for you.

Is there a contract or minimum term?

No. With TravelPerk you are free to join or leave whenever you like without any ties, though we love our product, and we think you will too.
