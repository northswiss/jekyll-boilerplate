Your company, your plan

How does the free version work?

When you book your travel using TravelPerk, we earn an affiliate commission, meaning that we are paid a small percentage of the cost of travel from the airline or hotel, meaning there is no additional cost or fees for you.

Are there any contracts or agreements?

No. We believe that the quality and value of our product is enough for you to want to use it without being forced in to any long-term commitments.
