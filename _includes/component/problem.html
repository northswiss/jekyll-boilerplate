How Travel Booking looks today

  • Fixed budgets

    Travel costs are not fixed, so why should your travel booking be? Having a fixed travel budget could mean that you pay much more during off-peak times or when travelling to cheaper destinations. Our Dynamic Budget helps you to save.

  • Too many tools

    With TravelPerks intuitive interface, you can create your own travel plans, create trips for colleagues and communicate all the details from within TravelPerk. One log in, one place for all of your business travel (including travel documents and itineraries).

  • No incentives to save

    With TravelPerk, our dynamic real-time budgets can save your company up to 30% on regular travel costs. In addition to this, you can also set up TravelPerk rewards which will incentivise staff to make further savings on their travel plans.
