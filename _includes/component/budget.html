Dynamic budgets

TravelPerk revolutionizes the way organizations manage and control travel expenses. Through dynamic real-time budgeting of business trips, companies no longer face the challenge of setting arbitrary budgets for business trips that do not reflect the reality of fluctuating market prices. Employees no longer need to worry about “breaking the budget” because they happen to be traveling during peak season and employers no longer need to worry that money is being spent by employees on unnecessary luxuries. With TravelPerk, employees spend their company’s money as if it were their own. IT’S A WIN-WIN!

  • Over 500,000 accommodation prices checked using Booking.com
  • All major global airlines and low cost carriers prices
  • Budget is customised to each users travel policy, giving maximum flexibility

Global coverage & flexible policies

When searching for your business travel, TravelPerks DynamicBudget™ is created by checking your dates and times of travel against live data giving you an accurate budget to work with.
